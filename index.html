FANGKNIGHT is a roleplaying game of gothic horror, treachery, and cursed nobility.
It can be played straight from the book, used to seed your own sinister world, or carved apart
into pieces and rewoven into your existing campaigns. The rules blend ritual simplicity with
baroque depth—feasting on fantasy while bleeding it dry.
